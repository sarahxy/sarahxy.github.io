What is Cognitive Science?
Cognitive Science is the interdisciplinary study of the mind and its processes through disciplines like psychology, linguistics, computer science, philosophy, neuroscience and anthropology.
I like using the story of the six blind men and the elephant to explain my major: the six blind men approach the mysterious elephant, touch different parts of the beast, and try to conceptualize what the elephant is like based on their encounters. Likewise, Cognitive Science tackles the challenge of understanding the mind by drawing upon other perspectives in order to better model the mind. (Currently, the mind still remain rather inscrutable to us because we are using our own minds to study and formulate ideas about what our mind is like!)
