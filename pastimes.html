Pastimes and Publications
I love to write. View some of my past works below!
Figment
A collection of short short stories and story ideas from my Figment account. Many were entries into Figment's various short story contests that were runner-ups for the prizes, notably "Ravenous Earth" and "Of Sunshine and Starlight".
"One Child"
A retelling of a story about a family under China's one-child policy, first drafted in a high school creative writing class. Editor's Choice Award.
"Waters of Miratha"
The adventure of the tree sprites Raen and Kiria as they seek to heal their mother tree from the waters of Miratha. First place in the Teen category of the 22nd Annual Short Story Contest.
