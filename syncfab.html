In the summer of 2016, I took on a UI/UX internship at SyncFab, a product that streamlines business transactions between local manufacturers and buyers. Our student team tackled many different design challenges, and had the opportunity to visit local manufacturers in order to better understand their processes.
User Research
We created and implemented a user testing session of the older website platform. Our team invited a user and set out a few tasks for him to complete (e.g., running through the login process, finding certain information on the website, etc.), and asked questions as we observed how he interacted with the product. I learned to interpret user behavior, as our subject's verbal feedback surprisingly did not often match his actions.
